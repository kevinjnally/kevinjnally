About Me

I am an architectural engineering student at Drexel University, transitioning to graduate studies in Civil Engineering at Villanova University. My focus is on structural engineering, balancing technical precision with an appreciation for the built environment.

When I am not working on structural analysis or researching mass timber construction, I am usually outside with a Fujifilm camera, training for endurance athletics, or preparing for my next mountain summit.
